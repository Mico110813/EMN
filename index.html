About

We are a not for profit organisation based in Edinburgh that is dedicated to improving the health and wellbeing of communities in Scotland. We achieve this through the delivery of excellent nutrition programmes and movement classes.

We have unique, holistic programmes to provide people with the tools, inspiration and knowledge to improve their health, lose weight, gain strength and feel great.

We are very happy to be part of the great work done by other like minded businesses such as; MacMillan Cancer Support, Edinburgh Leisure, the Edinburgh University Student Association and Classical Yoga School . If you have an idea as to how we can help your business or place of work then please get in touch today!

If you are looking to get happier and healthier then we would love to help you. If you are in the Edinburgh or Perthshire areas why not come to a class or book in for a more individually tailored solution that meets your current situation. If you are from elsewhere and are interested then please get in touch, maybe your area will be next!

Nutrition

The 21-Day Guided Mindful Nutrition Course is a program designed by us after careful consultation with fellow professionals and nutritionists and successful trials which provided great results and feedback. It will help you break addictions to sugar and processed foods, become more connected to the food chain and your environment, highlight areas where your diet could improve, help you to avoid common pitfalls such as yo-yo dieting and provide you with a tailored movement plan – while feeling healthier and losing weight.

At present we only cover Edinburgh and Perth. If you are from somewhere else get in touch and maybe your area will be next!


One to One

There is nothing like a one to one to get the motivation and skills required to make a big impact on your happiness and health.

These 1.5 hour sessions are broken up into 2 sections; Nutrition advice and tailored movement training and have a strong focus on empowering you to learn enough to take the skills home and practice them
